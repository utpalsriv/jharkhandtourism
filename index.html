About

Jharkhand is 'the land of forests' - endowed with rich flora and fauna. To have a complete panoramic view of nature and beauty, one can visit the zoological gardens and national parks. The state is situated in the eastern part of India. In the year 2000, it was split from southern Bihar and was formed into another state.

The scenic beauty of the place is what makes it most attractive. Tourism in Jharkhand comprises of pilgrimages, national parks and other industrial townships. But the picturesque nature and mesmerising beauty of Jharkhand might leave you spell bound. Waterfalls, lakes and dams in Jharkhand are favourite hub for photographers.
